# Still

A companion for lifelong journaling that remembers what endured.

## The idea

Most journaling tools remember what happened.

Still tries to remember what endured.

Instead of summarizing entries, Still looks across years of notes to surface recurring threads, values, fears, strengths, and forms of self-reassurance that persist through change.

The goal is not advice.

The goal is recognition.

## Example

A traditional journal app might say:

> In 2015 you felt lost and uncertain. In 2018 you felt confident.

Still asks a different question:

> What remained true across both moments?

The answer might be:

> When uncertainty appears, you repeatedly become your own source of reassurance.

That is not a memory.

It is a thread.

## Principles

* Remember what endured, not what happened.
* Surface one observation, not a dashboard.
* Evidence before interpretation.
* Recognition over advice.
* Privacy by default.

## Status

Early prototype built from a decade of personal journals.

The current focus is developing a reasoning engine that can identify enduring threads across years of writing while avoiding shallow summaries and manufactured insights.
