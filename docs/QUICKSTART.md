# Yadegar — Quick Start

*Yadegar is a quiet companion to journaling. Write your pages (or bring in years of old ones), and Yadegar helps you return to what endured — always in your own words. The full guide is in [FAQ.md](./FAQ.md) or in the app under **Help & FAQ**.*

## 1. Get in
- Sign up with **email + password** or **Continue with Google**.
- Pick what fits on the welcome screen: bring in old journals, start writing, start fresh, or try sample pages.

## 2. Write a page
- Open **Today** and write. It **auto-saves** ("saving…" → "saved").
- Press **"Keep this page →"** to file it and start a new one.
- Optional: **format** the text, add a **photo** (＋ Add image), or tap the **🎙 mic** to dictate.

## 3. Bring in past journals (optional)
- Go to **Import**, paste your journal or upload a `.txt` / `.md` / `.docx` (Google Docs: File → Download → Word).
- Review the detected pages and dates, then **Keep** them. They land in your **Library**.

## 4. Let a page return to you
- On Today, press **"✦ Bring a page back."** Yadegar reads across your archive and returns **one** thing worth revisiting — or honestly says nothing surfaced.
- Everything it returns is saved under **Returns**.

## 5. Look back on purpose
- **Look back →** browse your past by theme (*What keeps returning*), by time (*Revisit a time*), or as a keepsake (*Your Year in Pages*).
- **Explore →** your **Library**, **Shelf**, **Collections**, and **Capsules**.

## 6. Make it yours
- **Reflect on this page** to write a note to your past self.
- **Favorite (★)** pages, add them to a **Collection** or your **Shelf**, or **seal a Capsule** for a future you.
- In **Settings → What returns**, choose how much Yadegar may resurface (Open / Gentle / Protected) and mute hard seasons.

## Good to know
- **Private by design:** your pages are encrypted at rest; the AI reads them only to choose what to return, never to train or share.
- **Silence is a feature:** Yadegar would rather say nothing than surface something false or wounding.
- **Yours to keep or leave:** export everything (Markdown / text / JSON) or delete your account anytime in **Settings → Privacy**.

→ Full details: **Help & FAQ** in the app, or [docs/FAQ.md](./FAQ.md).
