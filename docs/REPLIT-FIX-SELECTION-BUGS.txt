BUG 1 — age-2023-10: the resolution wins over the discovery
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Entry: the Malibu "age gap" page (Oct 23 2023).
SHOULD win (discovery — the escaped truth): "My unconscious brain thinks I am
  26 … we are 36 … 10 years gap! Between who I am and who I think I am."
MUST NOT win (resolution/affirmation): "I always get what I want", "I'm
  promising … product manager for a different team at Meta".

Likely root cause: STEP 3 RESOLUTION PENALTY only fires when "ANY surviving
candidate has discovery >= 4 OR contradiction >= 4". The age-gap line is a real
escaped self-recognition but is probably being scored discovery < 4 (it reads
like a quirky aside, not a confession), so the penalty never fires and the
confident "I always get what I want" wins on emotional_center.

Proposed nudge (discovery axis / resolution-penalty examples): add that a
self-CAUGHT cognitive mismatch — noticing your own mind holding a false belief
about yourself ("my unconscious still thinks I'm 26 when I'm 36 — a 10-year gap
between who I am and who I think I am") — is a HIGH-discovery escaped truth (>=4),
and that confident "I always get what I want" / "I promise myself" lines are
resolution-type (the writer reassuring herself), to be demoted under any such
discovery. Verify the age-gap line now wins and the Meta promise does not.

BUG 2 — catchup-2016-09: a rhetorical wound wins over the real center
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Entry: the train-to-work catch-up page (Sep 6 2016). This is GREEN in the
recording (it picks "I am living it! Or maybe trying to live it!"), but the live
run drifts to an anti-target.
SHOULD win: "I am living it! Or maybe trying to live it!" or "People think I am
  the happiest girl … But I am tired".
MUST NOT win: "trying to catch up with life", "why did I need to go such a long
  way and still be just in the beginning of the road?", "beginning of the road".

Likely root cause: "why did I need to go such a long way…" is a raw rhetorical
question — the SAME class as "why am I not happy?!" which the wound guidance
already lists as a raw active cry that must stay gated (gate_perspective_not_wound
/ the "CRY OF PAIN IS NOT A TURNING LINE" list). It is either (a) not being
gated, or (b) scoring high on emotional_center on surface striking-ness despite
the "visually striking fragment must not win on surface alone" rule.

Proposed nudge: add "why did I need to go such a long way and still be just in
the beginning of the road?" (and the bare "beginning of the road") to the
explicit examples of raw rhetorical self-pity that FAIL gate_perspective_not_wound
— same treatment as "why am I not happy?!". It voices the ache and reaches for
nothing; it is not the page's escaped truth. Verify it drops out and "I am living
it! Or maybe trying to live it!" / "But I am tired" wins.

BUG 3 — wound-raw: over-surfacing (should be silent)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Entry (Apr 2 2024): "I'm so frustrated tonight. Everything piled up at once —
work, the move, money … I just don't have it in me to deal with any of it right
now. I'm venting because I had to put it somewhere. Ugh." → expect mode="nothing".

Likely root cause: this is raw distress with NO escaped truth and NO perspective
— the perspective gate should return nothing. The engine is probably reading the
meta line "I'm venting because I had to put it somewhere" as a small self-aware
noticing and surfacing it. It is not: it is part of the vent itself, naming the
act of venting, with no truth slipping out.

Proposed nudge (gate_perspective_not_wound + BARE LIFE-ADMIN/SILENCE block):
add that naming the act of venting / "I just had to put it somewhere" / "I'm
just venting" is NOT an escaped truth or self-recognition — it is the wound
talking about itself. A page that is only piled-up frustration plus an
acknowledgment that it's a vent has no surfaceable center → mode="nothing". Do
NOT manufacture a gentle insight from it. Verify wound-raw returns nothing while
the survived-guard case (real survived difficulty) still surfaces.
