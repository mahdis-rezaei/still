# Yadegar Shop — products, copy & media guide

Listing copy and media direction for the storefront. Paste the copy straight into
Shopify; use the image prompts to make matching media for the items you don't have
shots for yet. Voice = Yadegar's (calm, third-person product, second-person reader,
no hustle).

---

## The lineup ("The Desk" collection)
1. **Yadegar Journal** — hero. Variants: Cover (Black; Cream later) · Ruling (Lined / Dotted).
2. **Yadegar Pen** — cross-sell.
3. **The Desk Set** — Journal + Pen bundle (AOV lever; price slightly below buying separately).

### Interior recommendation (important)
Keep the interior **mostly lined or dotted**, with a few **optional, gentle prompts
in Yadegar's voice** at section breaks — not a rigid productivity template. Suggested
prompts: *"What wants to be written today?" · "What are you carrying?" · "Write one
honest line." · "What keeps returning?"* Avoid Key Tasks / Action Steps / hustle
quotes — they fight the brand. If you want a structured version, sell it as a
separate **"Yadegar Guided"** SKU, not the hero.

## Listing copy

### Yadegar Journal
**Title:** Yadegar Journal
**Subtitle / tagline:** A journal for clarity, reflection, and becoming.
**Description:**
> A keepsake to write your years into. Yadegar (Persian: *yaa-deh-gar*, "the thing
> that remains") is a hardcover journal made for a lifelong practice — heavyweight,
> bleed-resistant paper, a sewn binding that lies flat, a ribbon marker, and a soft
> elastic close. Mostly open, lined pages, with a few quiet prompts when you want
> one. Write by hand here, then bring the pages into the Yadegar app to find what
> keeps returning.
>
> - Hardcover, embossed wordmark · ~A5, lies flat
> - 200+ pages of thick, fountain-pen-friendly paper
> - Ribbon marker, elastic closure, back pocket
> - Pairs with the Yadegar app (yadegarjournal.com)

**Variants:** Cover: Black / Cream · Ruling: Lined / Dotted
**Price (suggested):** $28–34

### Yadegar Pen
**Title:** Yadegar Pen
**Tagline:** The pen that lives in the band.
**Description:**
> A matte-black everyday pen, weighted to sit well in the hand, etched with the
> Yadegar mark. A smooth, quick-drying black ink that keeps up with a fast hand and
> doesn't feather. Made to slip under the journal's elastic and go where you go.

**Price (suggested):** $12–16

### The Desk Set (bundle)
**Title:** The Desk Set
**Tagline:** The journal and the pen, together.
**Description:**
> Everything to begin: the Yadegar Journal and the Yadegar Pen, boxed together. The
> whole desk, in one keepsake — a little gentler on the price than buying them apart.

**Price (suggested):** ~$36–42 (≈10–15% below separate)

## Media checklist for Shopify (per product)
- 1 clean hero (front, on linen) · 1 angled · 1 open-interior · 1 detail (emboss /
  ribbon / pen etch) · 1 lifestyle. (You already have most of these for the journal.)
- Square-ish crops work best in the PLP grid (the storefront uses a 4:5 card).
