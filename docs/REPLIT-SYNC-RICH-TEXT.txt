1. Rich text (f4ea4af): a literary formatting layer on journal pages — bold,
   italic, two heading levels, bullet/numbered lists, blockquote, and a few
   muted on-brand text colors — in BOTH the Today composer and the entry view.

   in Object Storage; never sent to the engine, never resurfaced.
